It's foggy, it's cold, you're a dad in Berlin, what else to do than to visit exhibitions?

I have visited three, that I'd like to mention and give a shout out to. Two together with Felix, to show him around. 

### Bevery Buchanan [@Haus am Waldsee][HaW]
Thought provoking and yet again art, you wouldn't see anywhere else in Berlin! Felix and I had a good time and as always had a coffee at the nice cafe.

### Carolyn Lazard [@Trautwein Herleth][TH]
So good friends, Felix and I had a good time and always such great exhibitions! Yet again a must see show.

![Alt texsst](/assets/images/20251012_Carolyn_Lazard.JPEG)
